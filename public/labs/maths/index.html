Exam-hall tactics

No negative marking

Eight of these papers carry no penalty for wrong answers — never leave a bubble blank. Eliminate two options and guess.

Convert units first

km/h → m/s is ×5/18; m/s → km/h is ×18/5. Convert before you compute and half the train/speed errors vanish.

Plug the options in

For "find x" questions, testing the four options is often faster than solving — especially in algebra and ages.

Ratio = parts

Turn every ratio into "parts", find one part, then scale. It collapses ratio, mixture and partnership into one method.

Odd days for calendars

Only the remainder after dividing by 7 matters. 100 days later is just (100 mod 7) = 2 days later.

Read DI labels twice

Pie segments are sometimes degrees, sometimes counts. Confirm which before you scale to the total.
