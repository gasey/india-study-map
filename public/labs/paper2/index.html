Original source — Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC)

All ten booklets are official MPSC Non-Gazetted past papers (Combined UDC, LDC, Sub-Inspector of Excise, ASI, Junior Accounts Officer, Assistant Audit & Accounts Officer, Combined Group-B). The commission publishes past papers and provisional answer keys free on its site:

  • OfficialPast papers (Non-Gazetted, 2024–2026): mpsc.mizoram.gov.in/page/old-question-ng
  • OfficialPast papers (2023–2025): mpsc.mizoram.gov.in/page/old-question-2023-2024
  • OfficialAnswer keys (Non-Gazetted): mpsc.mizoram.gov.in/page/answer-key-ng
  • OfficialSyllabus (Non-Gazetted): mpsc.mizoram.gov.in/page/syllabus-ng

Third-party mirrors also host these papers (usually behind sign-ups or ads) and often add solutions and mock tests:

  • MirrorTestbook — Mizoram PSC previous-year papers & mock tests: testbook.com/mizoram-psc-ldc/previous-year-papers
  • MirrorSolvingPapers — govt past papers PDF: solvingpapers.com/mizoram-psc-previous-year-question-papers
  • MirrorFreshersNow — Mizoram PSC ASI papers: freshersnow.com/mizoram-psc-asi-previous-question-papers
  • MirrorToppersExam — Mizoram PSC MCQ question bank: toppersexam.com — Mizoram MPSC question bank

Why questions here are typed out by hand rather than auto-scraped: the official site blocks automated downloads, and bulk-copying question text from third-party sites raises copyright issues and drags OCR errors along with it. So the practice questions marked from papers were transcribed and re-solved from your uploads, and the ones marked new were written fresh on the same patterns and difficulty — that way the bank can grow without limit and every answer is verified. For the complete, unedited papers, use the official links above.
